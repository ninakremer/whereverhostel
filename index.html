(OUR) tours

Arctic Adventures

( Wherever Mini Hostel )

N°01

Chase the
Northern Lights

Join a small-group Northern Lights hunt from Rovaniemi, focused on giving you the best possible chance to see the aurora in a calm, personal setting. On the day of the tour, you will be informed by 16:00 whether the tour will run, as the decision depends on weather and solar activity. If the Northern Lights do not appear during the tour, you receive a full refund. Travel with an experienced guide in a comfortable vehicle and enjoy an authentic Arctic night away from the crowds. Professional photos included.

(PRICE) €129 per person

Book a Tour
N°02

Visit
Korouma Canyon
& Riisitunturi National Park

Visit two of Lapland's most iconic national parks, Korouma Canyon and Riisitunturi, in one day and save your travel time. This small-group tour runs from 8:00 to 16:30 and includes guiding, photos taken by us, and a warm meal with drinks, so you can simply enjoy the landscapes without any stress. Perfect for travellers who want frozen waterfalls, snowy forests, and the best Arctic scenery in one well-planned day.

(PRICE) €180 per person
